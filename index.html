About InternLink Ghana

InternLink Ghana is an innovative digital platform designed to bridge the gap between students seeking practical internship experience and companies searching for talented interns. Our mission is to empower Ghanaian youth by connecting them with meaningful internship opportunities, fostering professional growth, and supporting organizations in building the next generation of skilled professionals. With a focus on transparency, accessibility, and user-friendly design, InternLink Ghana is dedicated to making internship placements seamless and impactful for both students and organizations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for an internship?

Go to the Internships page, select an opportunity, and apply through the detail page. If a CV is required, you will be prompted to upload it.

How do companies post internships?

Register as a company, log in, and use the "Add Internship" button on your dashboard. Your listing will be visible to everyone.

Are internships visible to everyone?

Yes! All listings are public on the Internships page.

Is InternLink Ghana free?

Yes, registering and applying for internships is free for students and companies.
